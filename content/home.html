FAQ

Questions worth asking

What is Serendib Authenticate?

Serendib Authenticate is The Merchant's proprietary four-pillar verification framework, designed to give buyers complete confidence in what they're purchasing. It is how we hold ourselves and our supply chain accountable.

How is Ceylon cinnamon different from cassia?

Ceylon cinnamon has a finer, more complex flavour; lighter, warmer, and slightly citrusy, without the harsh bite of cassia. Structurally, Ceylon forms thin, papery quills with multiple layers; cassia is a single, thick bark roll. Chemically, Ceylon contains trace levels of coumarin, while cassia contains significantly higher levels; a concern for anyone consuming cinnamon in meaningful quantities, particularly in supplements, beverages, or daily cooking.

Where does The Merchant's cinnamon come from?

Our cinnamon is sourced directly from heritage-growing regions in Sri Lanka. We trace each batch to its origin and maintain documentation of provenance as part of our Serendib Authenticate verification process.
